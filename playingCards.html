Standard 2-color 52-card deck with Jokers

Four suits, 13 cards each, high to low: A, K, Q, J, 10, 9, 8, 7, 6, 5, 4, 3, 2.

Standard “French” suit names in bold. Suits ranked from high to low as in Bridge.

Black Joker, White (or Red) Joker, and Card Back are also included.

Spades: 699 Rules

52 cards using 2 Joker cards. Discard Two of Clubs and Two of Hearts from deck before deal. Two of Diamonds becomes Third Joker.

Big Joker (Black/Colored), Little Joker (White/Plain/Red), Two of Diamonds, Two of Spades leads Spades trump suit (16 cards).

Remaining three suits have 12 cards each.
